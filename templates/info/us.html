United States - Tree Planting

About the United States

The United States is home to a vast array of ecosystems, from temperate forests to coastal wetlands. Tree planting efforts play a critical role in protecting these diverse habitats and fighting climate change.

By supporting tree planting initiatives in the U.S., you contribute to the preservation of these ecosystems and the fight against environmental challenges.

Types of Trees

The U.S. is known for a variety of iconic tree species, including:

  • Oak
  • Redwood
  • Pine
  • Maple
  • Willow

Planting these trees helps improve air quality and support local wildlife.

How to Plant Trees

Help support tree planting in the U.S. by donating to these organizations:

  • Arbor Day Foundation
  • One Tree Planted
  • Trees Atlanta
