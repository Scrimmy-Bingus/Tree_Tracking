About Angola

Angola, located in Southern Africa, is known for its diverse landscapes, including tropical Atlantic beaches, a labyrinthine system of rivers, and the Sub-Saharan desert. The country is rich in natural resources, particularly oil and diamonds, and has a vibrant cultural heritage.

Vegetation in Angola

Angola features a variety of vegetation, from tropical rainforests to savannas. Key species include:

  • Baobab
  • Mopane
  • Miombo Woodland
  • Acacia
  • Palm Trees
  • Ebony
  • Mahogany
  • Grasslands
