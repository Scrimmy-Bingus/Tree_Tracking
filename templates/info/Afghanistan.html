Afghanistan

About Afghanistan

Afghanistan, a landlocked country in South Asia, is characterized by rugged mountains, arid deserts, and fertile valleys. The Hindu Kush range dominates its geography, providing vital water sources for the region. Its ecosystems support unique biodiversity, including rare species like the snow leopard and Marco Polo sheep.

Vegetation in Afghanistan

Afghanistan features a range of vegetation, from alpine meadows to desert plants. Key species include:

  • Juniper
  • Pistachio
  • Wild Almond
  • Walnut
  • Poplar
  • Tamarisk
  • Wild Olive
  • Artemisia
