About Guyana

Guyana, located on the northeastern coast of South America, is home to some of the most biodiverse rainforests in the world. Its rich ecosystems support countless species of flora and fauna, making it an essential location for environmental preservation efforts.

Vegetation in Guyana

The vegetation in Guyana includes various types of forests, from rainforests to savannahs, all contributing to its rich biodiversity. Key plant species include:

  • Greenheart
  • Mahogany
  • Rosewood
  • Baromalli
